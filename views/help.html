How to help Us

HOW YOU CAN HELP

There are several ways that you can help Good Samaritan Foundation, Haiti Mission accomplish their mission. Here's just a few.﻿ If you have any questions concerning our programs, please feel free to contact us, we would be very happy to help you. We welcome you as you’d like to partner with us to help those living in extreme poverty in Haiti

Become An Intern - Serve your internship with Good Samaritan Foundation, Haiti Mission.

  
Lead Sponsorship Drive﻿- Help us get sponsors for our orphanage children by spreading the words to your church, your co-workers, your classmate, your family, or your friends.

Project- Help Good Samaritan Foundation, Haiti Mission builds a larger orphanage for 100 street children in Haiti

Volunteer - Get involved by volunteering. You can become a team member and assist with cataloging and packing donated items, accounting, calling, mailers and many other ways. Extra hands make it so much easier for everyone.

Fundraising - Start and hold a fundraiser in your community, your church, or your family to raise funds to sponsor a food distribution in Cite Soliel for over 500 families living in extreme poverty or to hold a Mobile Medical Clinic

Become An Ambassador - Be an ambassador who spreads the word about Good Samaritan Foundation, Haiti Mission to your church, your school, your work, your friends, your community and your family.

Social Network Partner - Join Good Samaritan Foundation, Haiti Mission in spreading the word through social networks. Whether you are on Twitter, Facebook, Linkedin, or all of them, you can help GSF, Haiti Mission find friends and make connections to make the GSF, Haiti Mission grow every day.

  
Donate - Your donations make our efforts possible. Your donations will be used to purchase food, medical supplies, project supplies, shipping, transportation, and other operational needs. We always make sure to send donators receipts and pictures

Sponsor - Sponsor a child, a Student, a Food Distribution, a Feeding Program, or a Mobile Medical Clinic.


Above all, we need your prayer for the spiritual life for those children, and your support. All of those items can be sent to Good Samaritan Foundation, Haiti Mission, Lake Arbor Drive, Palm Springs, FL 33461
